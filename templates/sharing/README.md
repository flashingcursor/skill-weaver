# Sharing Templates

This directory contains ready-to-use templates for announcing and distributing the weaver-create to your team or organization.

## Available Templates

### Email Templates
- **[email-announcement.md](email-announcement.md)**: Professional email announcement for teams

### Chat Templates
- **[slack-announcement.md](slack-announcement.md)**: Slack/Microsoft Teams announcements with multiple formats

## How to Use These Templates

1. **Choose the right template** for your communication channel
2. **Customize placeholders**:
   - Replace `[Team/Name]` with actual names
   - Update `[Date/Time]` with your training session schedule
   - Change `[Slack channel or email]` to your support channel
   - Add your organization-specific information
3. **Personalize examples** to match your team's use cases
4. **Test the links** to ensure they point to the right resources
5. **Send or post** according to your distribution plan

## Customization Tips

### For Small Teams (< 10 people)
- Use the simple email template
- Skip formal training sessions
- Offer one-on-one help as needed

### For Medium Teams (10-50 people)
- Use both email and Slack announcements
- Host a single training session
- Create a support channel

### For Large Organizations (50+ people)
- Phased rollout with pilot group
- Multiple training sessions
- Dedicated support resources
- Track adoption metrics
