## Tips for Slack/Teams Posts

1. **Use emojis** to make posts visually engaging
2. **Pin important announcements** in relevant channels
3. **Create a dedicated channel** for AI tools if you have many users
4. **Post at optimal times** (Tuesday-Thursday mid-morning works well)
5. **Follow up** with tips and success stories
6. **Encourage questions** in threads
7. **Share examples** that are relevant to your team's work
